A long time ago I was looking for a domain name and finally settled on securso.com. Mainly because securso is an anagram of Scouser. A Scouser is someone who was born and bred in Liverpool, England, which was where I was born.

Several years ago, I decided scouser.com was too expensive, and that souser.uk would suffice. So, I acquired .uk and dropped .com.

A few years ago, I was looking for a new domain name, and found that retirednotout.uk was available. I was very, very surprised that it was, so I grabbed it. After all I’m a retired programmer who refuses to give up programming.

I no longer have a website associated with the domain name.

My website and blog now uses retirednoutout.uk, which is here.
